My Dearest Vanessa, From the quiet corners of my heart Where your name echoes softly like a prayer, I gather every feeling, every spark, And weave them gently into words for you. Vanessa, You are the sunrise that paints my darkest skies, The gentle hush that calms my restless nights, The warmth that lingers in my trembling hands When the world feels far too cold. Before you, Love was just a word I’d heard in songs, A passing whisper in crowded rooms. But with you, Love became a heartbeat I could feel, A steady rhythm calling me home. I love the way your laughter Falls like silver bells in the morning air, How your smile bends the light around you And makes even ordinary days glow. When you look at me, It feels like the universe pauses As if every star leans closer Just to witness your grace. In your eyes, I see tomorrow Not as something distant or unknown, But as a promise waiting to unfold. A thousand quiet mornings, Coffee growing cold between our hands Because we’re too busy talking, Too lost in each other’s stories. Vanessa, You are the calm in my chaos, The courage in my doubts, The dream I never knew I was brave enough to wish for. With you, I am softer. With you, I am stronger. With you, I am exactly who I want to be. If love were a garden, You would be the rarest bloom Not because you are delicate, But because you are resilient, Growing beautifully even through life’s storms. And I would spend every season Learning how to care for you, How to cherish you, How to love you better each day. On this Valentine’s Day, I don’t offer you diamonds or grand displays, Only this heart Honest, devoted, and entirely yours. A heart that chooses you In the quiet moments, In the loud ones, In the ordinary days and the extraordinary ones. I promise to stand beside you When the road is smooth And when it turns uncertain. To hold your hand not just in celebration But in challenge. To remind you, again and again, That you are loved beyond measure. Vanessa, my love, If I could write our story across the sky, I would spell it in constellations So the world would look up at night And see how brightly you shine in my life. You are my today, My tomorrow, My always. Happy Valentine’s Day, my beautiful Vanessa. Forever yours.
